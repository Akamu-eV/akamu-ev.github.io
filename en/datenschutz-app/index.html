Privacy Policy - Akamu App

as of March 25, 2022
Did you know that Akamu collects personal data from you? Akamu doesn't work without personal data.
Akamu stores your university, your username and how to reach you by email.
We use Firebase Crashlytics for automatic crash reports. Only if you want, you can agree to enable Crashlytics on your device at the end of our privacy policy.
Only with your consent to the Privacy Policy we can use your data legitimately. For questions and feedback email to datenschutz@akamu.de

What we store

We store your email address. Other users cannot see your email address.
Why?
To create your account. To reset your password if you forget it. To check your student status. To notify you of impmortant information such as an app update or a revision of your privacy settings.
We store your username. We don't store your real name. The username is visible to other users and depending on your choice may give a clue on your real name.
Why?
Other users can find you within Akamu and you can of course search for game partners. You cannot play Akamu without a username.
We store your university. Other users can see your university.
Why?
You can find questions from your university. You can network with users who are from the same university.
Akamu only uses the data you give us and doesn't share your data with third parties.
We don't access: camera, location, contact book, data about you from other networks

How we store

The data is sent encrypted to our servers according to TLS protocol, which also communicate with each other encrypted. The app as well as our servers are developed by us.
Where we store
Your data is well protected in our server locations in Nuremberg and Falkenstein. The servers are being operated by Hetzner Online GmbH.
How long we store
Up to one month after deletion of the account. The data processing begins with your registration.

What you can do

Revoke your consent
Request information about what we know about you, what we do with that data where and for how long.
Correct your data.
Restriction of data, e.g. the semester information.
Transfer of your data to another person responsible.
Delete - Your right to be forgotten
Complain to the competen supervisory authority.
To get more information or exercise your rights send us an email to datenschutz@akamu.de

Akamu reserves the right to change the privacy policy at any time - in compliance with legal requirements. In this case Akamu will inform you immediately. You can always find the privacy policy in the app settings.

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics for automatical crash reports. When an error occurs while you are using the app, a crash report will be created. The report gives us information about the occurance of the crash, date and time, information about your device and your operating system.
You can find the privacy policy of Firebase Crashlytics here.
By enabling Crashlytics you can help us make Akamu better.
